Query Closing Prediction on Stack Overflow (Q&A Website)











Problem

With over 13 million questions and 6 million users, Stack Overflow(SO) is a Question and answer website where computer scientists can post and answer questions. As the community has grown, the ratio of answers to questions has decreased by over 80 percent. Therefore, community moderators prevent content dilution(which is often a result of unfamiliarity of new users with SO guidelines) by closing questions that are of low quality citing reason (Duplicate, off topic, subjective, unclear or localized)for closing it as well.

Solution

In order to help new users get acclimated to the site and reduce moderator burden by automating quality control, our objective is to build a classifier to predict whether a userâ€™s question will be closed by the aid of machine learning algorithms.

Outcome

On the successful completion of the project, our model will be able to predict wether a question posted on SO should be closed or not, with significant accuracy. We further aim to predict the reason for closure of a question, if possible.


Project Udaan











Government schools in India lack in providing wholesome education to its students. One would generally 2-3 teachers in a government school who teach all the subjects to all the classes from third to eight grade in school. On the other hand, private schools house abundance of teachers and extra curricular activities that help in an all-round development of children.

Udaan (which translates to 'uplifting to greater heights') is a project under the youth led NGO Gramiksha, which focuses on developing personal and social qualities among children and promoting extra curricular activities in government schools by conducting 3 hour long Saturday Sessions in schools.

The Project is operative in over 6 cities in India- Indore, Bhopal, Gwalior, Delhi and Jaipur, and stretches over a period of 20 weeks, where students are engaged in activities like Sports, Science exhibitions, self defense workshops, public speaking, Art, Music, Theatrics and Dance. I, along with 30 other college students, have carried out the project in Government School Subhash Nagar located in Indore.


Money Matterns: Group Expense Manager











Problem

2019 passing out batch of Engineering students in college was surveyed about their group expense habits and it was observed that majority of the respondents generally went out to eat with friends or flat mates every week and 70% of those found it difficult to keep track of individual expenses.

Solution

Thus, we decided to develop a website where users can create an account and keep track of their expenses. Just create a group, add an activity, input the users involved, and amount paid by each.

Outcome

The website will then calculate who owes whom and how much. The locally hosted website is being used by over 60 users.
